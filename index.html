Flashback

Ubuntu Touch app to cover the entertainment needs of the the user (movies and tv shows).

Install it with:

sudo apt-add-repository ppa:ubuntu-touch-community-dev/ppa
sudo apt-get update
sudo apt-get install flashback







Introducing Flashback.

Flashback is an Ubuntu Touch application that helps you manage your movie and TV show collection.

It integrates with Trakt and TMDB online services to provide a variety of features such as discovering new movies, keep track of shows, adding movies and tv shows to your watchlist to watch later.

Currently supported features:
  • Discover trending movies and tv shows;
  • Quickly look through movies currently playing in theatres;
  • Add movies and tv shows to your watchlist;
  • Get a personalised list of episodes airing today based on tv shows you track;
  • Find out more about an actor/actress, their biography and movies they have acted in.
